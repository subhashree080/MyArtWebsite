About Me

I am an Indian artist born in Bhubaneswar,Odisha. I am a selftaught artist and I had a passion of colorpencil artwork.I am well versed with Jewellery Design art, Doodle arts, Crayon arts along with craft works using paper,quilling papers,woollen crafts. I have received my Post Graduation certificate in Master in Computer Application in 2020. I started drawing at the age of 10 and I sometimes think there is nothing so delightful as drawing.One must always draw,draw with eyes, when no one cannot draw with a pencil. Drawing is a vision on paper.And in my view Art enables us to find ourselves and lose ourselves at the same time.
